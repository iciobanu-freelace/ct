What we do

Vectrogrup SRL offers a comprehensive selection of services to help you manage and retrofit your power system equipment.

Design engineering

Engineering finalizes design details and oversees the project from award through completion. − Develop bill of material − Provide design − Project engineering − Evaluation − Coordination − Review customer specifications − Design reviews − Project management

On-site installation or retrofits

We will provide the necessary engineering, documentation and commissioning to meet our customer’s needs. Existing panels are modified and retrofitted at the customer’s location. Relay panels are installed and tested on-site by qualified Vectrogrup SRL relay service personnel.

Diagnostic and test instruments

Vectrogrup SRL supports our clients in the electric power industry improve operations and optimize system performance. Check relay schemes, evaluate insulation condition and assess risk across your transformer fleet with Doble, Fluke,Sonel and other diagnostic test equipment and software.
